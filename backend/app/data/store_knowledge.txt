Shipping Policy
Standard shipping usually takes 3 to 5 business days.
Express shipping usually takes 1 to 2 business days.

Return Policy
Customers can return eligible products within 7 days of delivery.
Returned items must be unused and in original packaging.

Refund Policy
Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after return approval.

Payment Methods
We support debit cards, credit cards, eSewa, Khalti, and cash on delivery.

Order Tracking
Customers can track orders using the order ID from the tracking page.

Customer Support
Support is available from Sunday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
